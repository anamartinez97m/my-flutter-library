# Privacy Policy for My Book Vault

**Last Updated**: November 28, 2025

## Introduction

My Book Vault ("the App") is developed and maintained by Ana Martínez Montañez. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data Stored Locally

My Book Vault stores all user data locally on your device. The App does NOT transmit any personal data to external servers. The following information is stored locally:

- **Book Information**: Titles, authors, ISBNs, page counts, reading dates, and personal notes you add
- **Reading Statistics**: Your reading progress, completion dates, and reading analytics
- **Custom Challenges**: Reading goals and challenges you create
- **App Preferences**: Theme settings, language preferences, and notification settings
- **Backup Files**: CSV export files you create (stored in your device's storage)

### Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

1. **Storage Access** (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)
   - Purpose: To import CSV files and export backup files
   - Usage: Only when you explicitly choose to import or export data

2. **Notifications** (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)
   - Purpose: To send reading reminders if you enable them
   - Usage: Only if you opt-in to notifications in the app settings

3. **Exact Alarms** (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM)
   - Purpose: To schedule precise reading reminder notifications
   - Usage: Only if you enable reading reminders

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.** All your data remains on your device.

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- No data is transmitted over the internet
- You have full control over your data through export and import features
- Data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Export**: Export your data to CSV format at any time
- **Delete**: Remove individual books or clear all data
- **Control**: Disable permissions at any time through device settings

## Data Retention

Data is retained on your device until you:
- Delete individual items within the app
- Clear the app's data through device settings
- Uninstall the application

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages and does not contain any age-restricted content.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
